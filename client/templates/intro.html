Everyone has something to say, your friends, family, business associates, and passive acquaintances included, and sometimes giving them a forum for shouting out every last cute puppy picture or lunch destination can seem like a bad idea.

If you manage several social media accounts and are getting tired of information overload from your hundreds of friends and followers, you need Dwibbles.

Dwibbles is a real-time communication platform that brings all of your social media feeds to one easy-to read, fully customizable page so you get only the updates that are relevant to you without having to change sites. Dwibbles acts as a content filter, empowering you to save time and annoyance while making it even easier to keep up with what’s important to you. Dwibbles provides you the functionality of all your social media accounts on one platform so that you can post and share content to all of your accounts from one easy to use source.

There’s nothing to lose but more time, so join Dwibbles today and stop jumping pages and drowning in dribble.
